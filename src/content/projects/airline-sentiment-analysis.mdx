## Problem

Airlines field huge volumes of public feedback on Twitter. Classifying the sentiment of
each tweet turns that noisy stream into a usable signal — for support triage and
reputation tracking.

## Approach

- **Data.** The Twitter US Airline Sentiment dataset (Kaggle), with tweets labelled
  positive, neutral, and negative.
- **NLP with NLTK** — text cleaning, tokenization, and stopword handling, feeding a
  sentiment classifier trained over the labelled set.

## My role & decisions

Built solo as an applied NLP exercise, taking the dataset end-to-end from raw tweets to a
trained sentiment classifier in a notebook.

## Outcome

A working sentiment classifier over the airline dataset, taken end-to-end from raw tweets
through cleaning and feature extraction to predicted positive / neutral / negative labels.
